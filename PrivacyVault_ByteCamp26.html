Problem Statement – DPDP Compliance Gap

With the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Rules 2025 notified Nov 13, 2025), organizations must ensure transparency, accountability, and citizen control over personal data — yet most remain dangerously unprepared.

  • ⚠️ Personal data scattered across disconnected systems — CRM, HR, analytics, Razorpay, delivery partners — with no unified view.
  • ⏱️ No centralized way to track data or respond to DSARs (Access, Correction, Erasure) within the mandatory 90-day window.
  • 🔗 Third-party processors retain copies — deletion requests go untracked, creating invisible liability.
  • 📋 Manual, Excel-based processes cause delays, errors, and high penalty risk — especially for breach notification failures.
₹250 Cr
Maximum Penalty Under DPDP Act
📊 90-Day DSAR Window
Organizations must respond to Access, Correction & Erasure requests within 90 days or face penalties. No current system tracks this automatically.
🌐 Fragmented Data Ecosystem
Average organization uses 5–12 third-party processors. Each holds personal data. None are coordinated for deletion propagation.
⚡ Nov 13, 2025 — Rules Notified
DPDP Rules 2025 are now in force. Enforcement pressure is immediate. Compliance tooling is urgently needed.

PrivacyVault Platform
PrivacyVault — Unified DPDP Compliance
Centralized Platform Operationalizing DPDP Compliance
  • 🗺️
    Data Mapping & InventoryMaps & tracks personal data across all internal systems and third-party processors in real time.
  • 👤
    Citizen Self-Service PortalCitizens can exercise rights (Access / Correction / Erasure) with verified identity — no calls, no paper.
  • ⚙️
    Admin Review Based WorkflowsAdmin queue with review, approval, and Email Token + Phone OTP verification before processing.

    Propagation to All ProcessorsDeletion/update commands pushed to all branches & processors with confirmation proof.
  • 🔍
    Request Tracking PortalCitizens track live DSAR status via unique token — shows progress stages, deadline, days remaining & verification state.
  • 📊
    Public Compliance DashboardLive compliance score, overdue count, avg. resolution days & request breakdown — full DPDP transparency report.
  • 📄
    Compliance-Proof PDF ReportsInstant audit-ready PDFs generated after each DSAR resolution for regulatory evidence.
